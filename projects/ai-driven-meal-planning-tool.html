AI-Driven Meal Planning Tool

TheAI Diet Planner is an web application built using HTML, CSS,JavaScript, Python And Inception V3.
This AI-powered diet planner utilizes image recognition technology to identify food items, determine their quantity, and calculate their calorie content.
By calculating an individual's Body Mass Index (BMI) based on their height and weight, the planner generates a personalized diet plan tailored to their specific needs.
For normal individuals, the planner provides customized meal plans that balance macronutrients and micronutrients to support overall health and wellness.
For diabetes patients, the planner takes into account the individual's dietary restrictions and medication, providing guidance on carbohydrate counting, glycemic index management, and healthy eating habits to help manage blood sugar levels.
By combining image recognition, BMI calculation, and personalized diet planning, this AI-powered tool offers a comprehensive approach to healthy eating and weight management for both normal individuals and those with diabetes.
